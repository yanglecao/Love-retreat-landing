Love Without Ownership

Relationships in the New Era | May 10–16, 2025

Overview

This retreat invites you to rediscover love as a source of nourishment—not pain. Let go of possessiveness, jealousy, and control. Embrace love as freedom, intimacy as connection, and relationships as soul resonance.

Who is this for?

  • People healing from heartbreak
  • Those tired of jealousy or control in relationships
  • Seekers of genuine, soulful, and free love
  • Anyone longing for a new paradigm of intimacy

Daily Flow (Sample)

  • Morning: Meditation, breathwork, nature walks
  • Midday: Teachings, group sharing, emotional healing
  • Afternoon: Trust games, eye gazing, embodied practices
  • Evening: Music, heart circles, forgiveness rituals
